Yan Shen (Yan Zhao)

I am a fourth-year PhD Candidate in Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies (CFCS) at Peking University, advised by Professor Hao Dong. Previously, I got my Bachelor degree from Yuanpei College, Peking University, in 2022.

My research interests include computer vision and robotics.

I used to go by the name Yan Zhao until the summer of 2023, when I changed it to Yan Shen.
